In Spring 2020, British GQ welcomes a new platform into its fold: GQ Editor's Club

We've worked tirelessly to expand the reach of our brand, from print to digital, to online engagement, and more recently, a packed events program that includes our latest innovation, GQ Heroes.

We're justifiably proud of the breadth of our activities and the respect they have earned from our friends, partners, and collaborators. That is why we feel it's time we bring you, our audience, closer to the heart of what we do.

Next April, we will launch the GQ Editor's Club. It's our chance to bring the brand to life, but more importantly, closer than ever to those who appreciate it most.

GQ Editor's Club has been created with you, the dedicated GQ reader in mind. It's a new community that will be cool, fun and inspirational, capable of connecting its members with the best possible business and lifestyle opportunities available.

It will also offer unique access to GQ's team of experts, with a carefully curated schedule of experiences covering everything from fashion and grooming to cars and travel, watches and collectibles to food, drink, health, and wellness.

I sincerely hope you'll join the conversation and I look forward to welcoming you to the GQ Editor's Club.

Dylan Jones, Editor-In-Chief, British GQ
